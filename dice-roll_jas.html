The rules are quite simple, really... it is kill or be killed. Well... maybe less dramatic. The object of the game is have a stroke of luck and make it to 100 points before your opponent does.

Here are the rules:

    - Whoever has the highest roll of the
    round wins +1 point.
    - If a player rolls a double AND wins
    the round, they get +2 points.
    - However if a player rolls a double
    AND does NOT win the round. Then
    they are not awarded +2 points and still lose.
    - If both players tie, then no one is
    awarded a point.
